Experience


Host & Cashier - Hibachi Grill - 2017-2021

- Hibachi Grill is a locally-run Chinese-American buffet in my hometown. In a small but mighty strip mall full of both mom 'n pop shops and major chains, Hibachi is certainly not for the weak, hosting dozens of dishes, along with a sushi bar and hibachi station for its customers. My position was not one either. As the primary host and cashier to the large establishment, it was my duty to direct guest flow to maximize efficiency and minimize wait time, manage transactions of party sizes reaching 100+ and checks reaching $1000+, and liaising management and client appointment and complaint resolution.
- Working at Hibachi Grill was quite a terrible experience, to be frank. Poor management, rude customers, mediocre food, so on and so forth. Despite that, Hibachi seemed to be a hub for many different people, all with only one thing in common: their love for that god- forsaken restaurant. I'm glad I quit.
