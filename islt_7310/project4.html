Experience

Cashier & Food Runner - McDonald's - 2016-2017

- I don't think this needs any introduction, but McDonald's Corporation is an American- based but international fast food chain. Founded in 1940 in California, McDonald's has become a household menu all across the globe, featuring everything from breakfast items like McMuffins and McGriddles to lunch items like Big Macs and Quarter Pounders to staples like fries and ice cream. My position as a cashier and occasional food runner was only a cog in the McDonald's machine, necessary to provide a high level of customer service during high-volume operations, mastery of the point-of-service computer system for taking orders, and handling transactions exchanges efficiently and accurately.
- Funnily enough, I believe that my experience at McDonald's was one that really shaped me as a person. Not necessarily a great experience, I came out of it with the firm belief that everyone should hold a food-service/retail position at least once in their lives. I still stand by that today.
