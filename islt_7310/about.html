Let me introduce myself.

If you told past-Alivia that future-Alivia would willingly decide to go to school for Information Technology instead of English or Music or Film or quite literally anything that's not STEM-related, then past-Alivia would laugh in your face. Who's laughing now?



Hello there. I'm Alivia Pham. With a BS in Information Technology in one hand and a minor in Film Studies in the other, it's now my turn to enter the workforce. When I was younger, I loved to exert creativity in every aspect of my life. Upon entering university, I decided to lean into the technicalities instead. Turns out, you can combine them both. After dabbling in a variety of aspects of IT (digital media design, coding, cyber security, etc), I'd love to pursue a career that allows me to use these freshly minted skills and put that creativity and technicality together.

As past-Alivia's idol used to say (Hannah Montana)...

you get the best of both worlds!
